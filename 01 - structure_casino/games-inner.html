Blackjack

The rules are simple, the play is thrilling, and there is opportunity for high strategy. In fact, for the expert player who mathematically plays a perfect game and is able to count cards, the odds are sometimes in that player's favor to win. Each participant attempts to beat the dealer by getting a count as close to 21 as possible, without going over it. Before the deal begins, each player places a bet, in chips, in front of him in the designated area.
